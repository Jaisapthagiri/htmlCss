Ben Tennyson, full name Benjamin Tennyson, is the main hero of the Ben 10 franchise created by Man of Action and first aired on Cartoon Network in 2005. At the age of 10, Ben discovers a powerful alien device called the Omnitrix, which attaches to his wrist and allows him to transform into various alien species, each with unique abilities. During his summer vacation, he fights villains alongside his cousin Gwen and Grandpa Max, protecting Earth from threats like Vilgax. As the series progresses through Ben 10: Alien Force, Ben 10: Ultimate Alien, and Ben 10: Omniverse, Ben grows from a fun-loving child into a more responsible teenage hero. The franchise later received a reboot in 2016, keeping the character popular worldwide through shows, movies, and games.
